Who is Blizzy?

Blizzy North, born in Fargo, ND, grew up between ND & MN. Blizzy's inspiration for music stems from a young age when his father went to prison. He discovered music such as Lil Wayne, Chamillionaire, Biggie, and more, which influences his southern style beat selection and unique punchline flow. Blizzy wrote, produced, and recorded his first song at the age of 11 and continued ever since.

Blizzy is the CEO of "CashFam" along with his fellow artist and day-one friend Crazy Flo. In 2019, Blizzy released his first official single and video "7 Feet Tall", with plans to release his first mixtape in early 2020.
